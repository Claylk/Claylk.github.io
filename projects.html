Trajectory Planning for Robotic Arm

Github Link

For the Robotics for Space Exploration design team (at the University of Toronto), I put together a ROS node that controls a mechanical arm mounted atop our rover.

The primary goal of the node is to reduce the arm's jerk when moving between two points, ensuring safety and durability. It does this by generating a polynomial curve for each motor that drives the arm, which has zero velocity at the end points.
